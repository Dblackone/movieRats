MERCH

Series to watch this quarantine

10. Bulletproof

  • Two Seasons
  • Sky One

Review- the show is about two cops pike and bishop who are best friends and how they solve crime in the streets of London. The first season wasn’t that impressive but the second season is why it is receiving a 4.6 and a recommendation. The show is excellent in portraying communication between the two leads and that conversation is often heart-warming and very funny. Would recommend.

9. Sick of it

  • Two Seasons
  • Sky One

Review – it is not everyday that you see a drama like karl pilkington’s sick of it. The show is about a man trying to get over a breakup and simply just taking life not too seriously. His closest friend is his inner self who talks boldly about the things that karl himself might not be able to say on his own. It is funny, heart-warming and excellently crafted. Would highly recommend.

8. His Dark Materials

  • One Seasons
  • Sky One

Review- if you are a fan of the golden compass the movie, this is a must watch for you. The show is about a girl lyra who is touted as a child of destiny and her adventures on realising this destiny. It involves a talking bear, an alethiometer ( a compass-like thing that can predict the future and find an answer to any question), witches and daemons ( the soul of everyone in this world has an animal representation called daemons). It’s a good watch and it will only get better

7. All Americans

  • Two Seasons
  • CW

Review – out of all the jargons that cw produced recently. All American is a standout drama and we had to put it on our list. The show is about a teenager who plays American football, his life and the people in his life. the show has a lot of heart and can be really emotional with topics ranging from sexuality to community. It’s a good watch and we highly recommend. Fun fact about this show. The guy that played spencer james is Daniel Ezra who is british but his American accent was really good
